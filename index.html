Eligibility & Requirements

College Requirement

All team members must be from the same college. Students of different courses but same college are allowed.

Gender Eligibility

Girls are not allowed to participate in this tournament. This is a boys-only competition.

Player Eligibility

Each player can represent only one team. Playing for multiple teams leads to disqualification.

Age Limit

Participants must be 18 + of age for the tournament.

Team & Match Format

Team Format

7 players + 3 substitutes per team; rolling substitutions allowed.

Match Time

10-minute halves; in case of a tie ‚Üí 3 penalties.

Throw-ins & Restarts

All throw-ins and outside balls must be taken from a grounded kick.

Gameplay Rules

Gameplay Rules

No offside rule; no slide tackling. Goalkeepers cannot throw the ball past the half line, cannot take volleys, and must release the ball within 4-6 seconds.

Discipline & Conduct

Discipline

Yellow card ‚Üí 3 minutes out; Red card ‚Üí suspended for current & next match.

Conduct

Only captains may speak to referees. Misconduct leads to team elimination.

Zero Tolerance

Any fights or serious misconduct will result in immediate team elimination.

Referee Decision

The referee's and committee decision is final and binding in all matters.

Protest Policy

Teams filing protests against opposition must pay ‚Çπ2,000 protest fee. The fee will be refunded only if the protest is valid. No refund if the protest is invalid or false.

Required Documents

Required Documents

Students must bring original Aadhar Card and College ID Card (current year) for verification.

Document Verification

Teams submitting false or invalid documents will be disqualified.
