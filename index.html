About the Tournament

‚öΩ The Soccer Knockout Challenge (SKC) is a premier inter-collegiate football tournament. Now in its fifth season, SKC stands as one of the most anticipated events across Navi Mumbai, Mumbai, and Thane, drawing top collegiate football talent. This year's edition is set to be bigger and better with enhanced facilities, increased prize money, and fierce competition promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and a platform for young players to shine.

Rules & Regulations

Follow the fair play spirit of the game!

Multiple Teams Allowed

Multiple teams from the same college are allowed to register in the event.

College Requirement

All team members must be from the same college. Students of different courses but same college are allowed.

Gender Eligibility

Girls are not allowed to participate in this tournament. This is a boys-only competition.

Player Eligibility

Each player can represent only one team. Playing for multiple teams leads to disqualification of team.

Age Limit

Participants must be between 18 and 24 years of age for the tournament.

Team Format

7 players + 3 substitutes per team; rolling substitutions allowed.

Match Time

10-minute halves; in case of a tie ‚Üí 3 penalties.

Required Documents

Students must bring original Aadhar Card and College ID Card (current year) for verification.

Document Verification

Teams submitting false or invalid documents will be disqualification of team.

Throw-ins & Restarts

All throw-ins and outside balls must be taken from a grounded kick.

Discipline

Yellow card ‚Üí 3 minutes out; Red card ‚Üí suspended for current & next match.

Conduct

Only captains may speak to referees. Misconduct leads to team elimination.

Zero Tolerance

Any fights or serious misconduct will result in immediate team elimination.

Gameplay Rules

No offside rule; no slide tackling. Goalkeepers cannot throw the ball past the half line, cannot take volleys, and must release the ball within 4-6 seconds.

Referee Decision

The referee's and committee decision is final and binding in all matters.
